The Challenges Farmers Face

Traditional farming communities in Odisha struggle with multiple barriers that limit their potential

Low Prices

Farmers earn low due to middlemen exploitation and lack of direct market access.

No Direct Access

No direct access to urban buyers and markets, limiting profit potential.

Crop Losses

Crop loss from pests, diseases, and unpredictable weather conditions.

Language Gap

Language barriers prevent tribal communities from accessing technology and information.

Lack Awareness

Limited awareness of government schemes and modern farming techniques.

Technology Gap

Lack of technology adoption in rural areas limits access to modern solutions.
